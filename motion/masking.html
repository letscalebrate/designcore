Masking

Masking allows designers to hide or reveal certain parts of an image. Masks are used across all areas of digital design and content creation: to cut out and combine different elements of a graphic or video, build transitions, form unique shapes, create awesome visual effects, and more.


The interaction above allows you to manipulate a mask path, using the four anchor points, to hide parts of the blue object in order to reveal the pink object underneath.
