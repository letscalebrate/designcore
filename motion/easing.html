Easing

Easing is one of the principles of animation that helps movements feel more natural and lifelike. To achieve this in a digital space, speed and value graphs are used to add acceleration and deceleration to objects on a screen.


The interaction above shows how manipulating easing curves can impact the motion of an object. Animations with easing principles applied, whether in digital products or in animated movies, feel more comfortable because they imitate what people see in real life.
