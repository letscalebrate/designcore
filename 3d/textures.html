Materials

A material determines the physical characteristics of an object like its color or translucency. Many properties go into making up a material such as the diffuse, roughness, bump, normals, and more. When on the object, they are meant to behave as the way a material does in the real world, casting shadows and reflecting light.


A big part of how a material will look is dependent on the way they are rendered and what type of render engine is used.
