Cloner & Effectors

A cloner is a type of 3D object that duplicates its contents. This allows for a clean and effective way of making multiple copies of something and being able to control them using effectors.

Effectors are modifications that can be applied to a cloner which will control all the contents at once. This can be extremely helpful if you are working with lots of objects and want to achieve a randomized or calculated look.

Take a look at all the ways effectors can altar objects and give it a try above by clicking on the screen to add a clone.
