Responsive Design

UI/UX stands for User Interface and User Experience Design. These two areas of digital design are often grouped together but have profound differences. They share the same visual space when it comes to digital spaces, but the ways in which they interact with users are different.


Wireframing and prototyping are two very common UX terms. They are both integral to the process of organizing the layout of a page for maximum efficiency. This interaction showcases a responsive wireframe. A wireframe is the skeleton of a digital design, and is a low fidelity technique to hash out bad ideas.


A big part of a product or website living online is the ability to be accessed from many different devices.
