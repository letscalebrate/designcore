Color Palette

Having an understanding of color theory is essential in the field of design. There are many ways to use color. The interaction above shows how different “color harmonies,” which are ways to pair colors together to be visually pleasing, can create many different palettes.


Artists and designers must consider aspects such as hue, saturation, value, and contrast when introducing color into their projects. Check out the resources below to learn about all of these terms and more about color theory.
