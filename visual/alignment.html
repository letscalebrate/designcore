Alignment

Alignment is a fundamental concept in design. The way you arrange text or elements has a big impact on how they are perceived. Good alignment means that all elements in a space feel cohesive and connected.


Alignment is only one of the core principles of design found in every good design work. Others include contrast, repetition, and proximity.
