Composition

Composition is the way your content is visually arranged. Composition is the base of design as it is the overall visual of your design.


Designers can use aides in composition such as grids. Some popular grids are seen in the interaction above such as the rule of thirds and the golden ratio. These grids assist in spacing visual elements to create focal points in designs.
